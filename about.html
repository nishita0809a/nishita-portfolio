About Me

My name is Nishita, and I’m a digital artist and designer currently studying in high school. I’ve always loved drawing and sketching, and over time I’ve expanded that passion into digital illustration, graphic design, and basic UI/UX design.

I enjoy combining hand-drawn creativity with modern tools to create polished, expressive visuals. My math and geometry skills help me with proportions, symmetry, and layout as I design characters, posters, and interfaces.

Interests

  • Digital illustration & character art
  • Graphic design & branding
  • UI/UX concepts and simple app layouts
  • Motion graphics and visual storytelling

Tools I Use

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Procreate, Canva, Figma, and traditional sketchbooks with pencil and ink.

Future Goals

I plan to study Graphic Design, UI/UX Design, Digital Media, or Illustration in college. My long‑term goal is to design visuals and interfaces that feel warm, clear, and inspiring for the people who use them.
